# Privacy Policy — Știai Că?

**Last updated:** July 7, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Știai Că? ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Facebook Page **Știai Că?** and the associated automation application **StiaiCa** registered on Meta for Developers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Facebook Page and application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or process any personal data from users of our Facebook Page.

The StiaiCa application is used exclusively by its administrator to publish automated posts to the Știai Că? Facebook Page. It does not interact with, collect, or store data from any other Facebook users.

## 3. How We Use Information

The application accesses only the following:

- **Page Access Token** — used solely to publish posts on behalf of the page administrator.
- **Page public data** — to verify the connection between the app and the page.

No user data is collected, shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the page:

- **Meta / Facebook** — for publishing posts ([Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/))
- **OpenAI** — for generating post content and images ([OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy))

## 5. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. The only data stored locally is the page access token used by the administrator, which is stored on a private server and never shared.

## 6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data from users, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about our Facebook Page, you can contact us via Facebook Messenger.

## 7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Facebook Page: **facebook.com/StiaiCa**

## 8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
